PROJECT SUMMARY: ALIEN SHED - SURVIVAL EDITION
Developer: Kuttlerj6796

Language: C++

Architecture: 2-File System (m5lab1.h and m5lab1.cpp)

1. OVERVIEW
An interactive text-based survival adventure where the player navigates a hazardous alien environment. The game features a persistent session-based scoring system, high-stakes combat, and hidden outcomes based on player health and luck.

2. CORE MECHANICS
HP System: Players start with 100 HP. Death occurs at 0 HP, triggering an immediate "Game Over" sequence.

Turn/Score Tracking: Every player decision increments a turns counter, which serves as the final score.

Persistent Stats: The program tracks Total Games Played and a Session High Score that persists until the application is closed.

Ranking System: Upon quitting, players are assigned a rank based on their best performance:

Galactic Legend (50+ turns)

Veteran Explorer (30+ turns)

Lucky Survivor (15+ turns)

Space Dust (<15 turns)

3. KEY LOCATIONS & EVENTS
The Path: Players are "nagged" via a loop and asteroid damage until they enter the shed.

The Glowing Goop: A high-risk gamble with three outcomes:

5% Critical Failure: Instant Death.

10% Miracle: Full HP restoration and "Super Soldier" status.

85% Standard: -40 HP damage.

The Alien Encounter:

Combat: A buffed boss fight (100 HP) featuring an "Enrage" phase at <40 HP, player Critical Hits (15% chance), and a tactical Dodge mechanic.

The Ninja Path: A rare 15% chance to sneak past the alien and avoid the "punt" damage entirely.

The Wall: Decoding the wall provides flavor text, unless the player has 100 HP, which triggers the Secret Ending (Luxury Escape) and a massive score bonus.

4. FILE STRUCTURE
m5lab1.h: Contains all inline global variables and full function definitions.

m5lab1.cpp: Contains the main() driver, the game loop, and the final session summary logic.
